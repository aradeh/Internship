Forest Carbon Facts

One acre of forest contains about 50 tons of CO2

Definitions of Carbon Pools
Aboveground live tree Live tree carbon that is above ground
Belowground live tree Live tree carbon that is above ground
Understory vegetation Carbon in the living vegetation that is not considered a tree, both above and below ground
Standing dead tree Biomass in standing dead trees
Forest floor Detritus on the forest floor including leaves, decaying material, and fine twigs
Downed dead wood Dead wood that is lying on the ground such as logs
Soil organic carbon Carbon in mineral soils
